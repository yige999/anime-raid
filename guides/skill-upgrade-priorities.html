üéØ End Game Priority Formula:

At max level, prioritize skills that provide the biggest percentage gains rather than flat bonuses. A 5% damage increase on a skill that deals 100,000 damage is worth more than a 100 damage flat bonus.

üé≤ Special Cases & Exceptions

üîÑ Hybrid Characters

Characters that fill multiple roles require special consideration:

  • Battle Mages: Balance damage and survival
  • Paladins: Tank + Support hybrid focus
  • Rangers: Ranged DPS + some utility
  • Ninjas: DPS + stealth utility

Rule: Identify your primary role and focus 70% on that, 30% on secondary role.

‚ö° Skill Synergies

Some skills have powerful interactions when leveled together:

  • Crit + Damage Skills: Exponential scaling
  • Heal + Defense Buffs: Multiplicative survivability
  • Debuff + Damage Skills: Amplified damage
  • Mobility + Attack Speed: Maximum uptime

Rule: Always level synergistic skills together for maximum effect.
