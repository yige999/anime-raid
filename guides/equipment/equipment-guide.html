⬆️ Equipment Enhancement Guide

🔧 Enhancement Methods

Level Enhancement

Basic enhancement using gold and materials

  • • Max level: +15
  • • Success rate: 100%
  • • Cost: Increases with level

Star Enhancement

Advanced enhancement using duplicate items

  • • Max stars: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
  • • Success rate: Variable
  • • Cost: Duplicate gear + materials

Attribute Enhancement

Customize secondary stats using special stones

  • • Max enhancement: +100 per stat
  • • Success rate: Decreases with level
  • • Cost: Attribute stones + gold

💎 Enhancement Costs & Success Rates

Level Enhancement Success Rates

+1 to +10:100% +11 to +13:90% +14:70% +15:50%

Protection Scrolls

Use protection scrolls to prevent gear loss on failed enhancement:

  • • Basic Protection: Prevents level loss
  • • Advanced Protection: Guarantees success
  • • Sources: Events, shop, achievements

💡 Pro Enhancement Tips

When to Enhance

  • • Wait until you have endgame gear
  • • Enhance during success rate events
  • • Save protection scrolls for +14 and +15
  • • Focus on main character gear first

Resource Management

  • • Always keep 20% of resources for emergencies
  • • Don't enhance temporary gear
  • • Buy enhancement bundles during sales
  • • Join gear enhancement events

🏗️ Optimal Gear Builds

⚔️ Max DPS Build

Ideal Characters: Flame Warrior, Shadow Assassin

Focus: Maximum damage output with high crit chance and damage
Weapon: Berserker Sword (ATK +25%, Crit DMG +15%)
Armor: Assassin Set (4-piece: Crit Rate +15%, Crit DMG +30%)
Accessory: Power Ring (ATK +10%, Crit Rate +8%)
Boots: Swift Boots (ATK SPD +15%, Movement +20%)
Enhancement Priority: Weapon > Armor > Accessory > Boots
Attribute Focus: ATK %, Crit Rate, Crit DMG

🛡️ Tank Build

Ideal Characters: Thunder Knight

Focus: Maximum survivability with team protection
Weapon: Guardian Hammer (DEF +20%, HP +15%)
Armor: Guardian Set (4-piece: DEF +20%, Team DMG Reduction -10%)
Accessory: Defense Amulet (DEF +10%, HP +8%)
Boots: Iron Boots (DEF +12%, Status Resistance +25%)
Enhancement Priority: Armor > Weapon > Accessory > Boots
Attribute Focus: DEF %, HP %, Status Resistance

💚 Support Build

Ideal Characters: Holy Priest, Ice Mage

Focus: Enhanced support abilities and survivability
Weapon: Holy Staff (Healing +25%, Energy Regen +15%)
Armor: Holy Set (4-piece: Healing +20%, Team Healing Radius +20%)
Accessory: Intellect Necklace (Healing +12%, Energy Regen +8%)
Boots: Support Boots (Energy Regen +10%, Movement +15%)
Enhancement Priority: Weapon > Armor > Accessory > Boots
Attribute Focus: Healing %, Energy Regen, HP %

⚖️ Balanced Build

Ideal Characters: Blood Count, Hybrid Characters

Focus: Balance between offense and defense
Weapon: Versatile Blade (ATK +15%, DEF +10%)
Armor: Battle Set (4-piece: ATK +10%, DEF +10%)
Accessory: Balanced Ring (All Stats +5%)
Boots: Combat Boots (ATK +8%, DEF +8%)
Enhancement Priority: Balanced progression
Attribute Focus: ATK %, DEF %, HP %
