🚀 Getting Started

Before You Start

1

Download & Installation

Download from official app stores only to avoid malware. Ensure you have enough storage space (2GB+).

2

Server Selection

Choose a server in your region for better ping. Check server population - avoid overcrowded or empty servers.

3

Account Setup

Link your account to email/social media immediately. This prevents losing progress if you change devices.

First Steps

1

Tutorial Completion

Complete all tutorial missions - they give generous rewards and teach essential mechanics.

2

Claim Pre-registration Rewards

Check your mail for pre-registration gifts and special launch rewards.

3

Join a Guild

Join an active guild early. Guild benefits include daily rewards, guild shop access, and mentorship from experienced players.

📅 Day 1-7 Progression Guide

1-2

Foundation Building

Main Objectives

  • • Complete main story quests (Chapter 1-3)
  • • Level up your starting character to Lv.20
  • • Clear normal mode stages 1-15
  • • Join an active guild
  • • Claim all daily rewards

Pro Tips

  • • Don't spend diamonds on summons yet
  • • Focus on one main character first
  • • Save energy refills for special events
  • • Upgrade equipment only for your main character
3-4

Team Building

Main Objectives

  • • Reach player level 25
  • • Build a 4-character team
  • • Unlock and clear hard mode stages
  • • Participate in first guild activities
  • • Start PvP arena matches

Pro Tips

  • • Build balanced team (1 tank, 1 healer, 2 DPS)
  • • Start saving summoning tickets for banner events
  • • Learn basic team compositions
  • • Upgrade shared equipment first
5-7

Advanced Progression

Main Objectives

  • • Reach player level 35+
  • • Clear first boss raid
  • • Participate in first weekly event
  • • Optimize team composition
  • • Start planning long-term progression

Pro Tips

  • • Research character tier lists
  • • Plan your resource investments
  • • Join community Discord for advice
  • • Save diamonds for essential purchases

⚡ Energy Management

Daily Energy Usage

  • • Main story progression (50% energy)
  • • Daily missions (30% energy)
  • • Event stages (20% energy)

When to Use Refills

  • • Double drop events
  • • Limited-time banners
  • • New content releases

💡 Essential Pro Tips

🎯 Gameplay Tips

  • • Auto-battle is great for farming but manual play is better for difficult content
  • • Learn enemy attack patterns to time your skills perfectly
  • • Positioning matters - keep ranged characters in back
  • • Save ultimate skills for boss phases or emergencies

🏆 Progression Tips

  • • Focus on one team rather than spreading resources thin
  • • Always check for daily and weekly missions
  • • Join active guilds for passive benefits
  • • Plan ahead for limited-time events

💸 Economy Tips

  • • Never spend real money on your first week
  • • Complete achievements for free premium currency
  • • Save resources for guaranteed character banners
  • • Use the marketplace wisely - sell unused items

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ What NOT to Do

Spreading Resources Too Thin

Don't try to upgrade every character. Focus on building one solid team first.

Ignoring Elemental Advantages

Elemental advantages provide 20% damage bonus - always check enemy elements.

Wasting Energy

Always use your energy before it caps out. Letting energy waste is losing progression.

✅ What to Do Instead

Focus Your Investments

Build one main team of 4-5 characters before expanding your roster.

Plan Ahead

Research upcoming events and banners to save resources accordingly.

Join Communities

Join Discord, Reddit, or Facebook groups for tips and advice from experienced players.
