⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes

Mistakes to Avoid

✗

Balance is key. A team without healers won't survive long content.

✗
Ignoring character synergies

Some characters work better together. Learn team compositions.

✗
Spreading resources too thin

Focus on building 3-4 core characters first.

✗
Not understanding role priorities

Know which roles are essential for each content type.
