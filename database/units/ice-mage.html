Active Skills

Frost Nova

Cooldown: 10s | Cost: 40 Energy

Releases a frost nova, dealing 150% attack power frost damage to all surrounding enemies with 80% chance to freeze for 2 seconds. Frozen enemies have 30% reduced defense.

Crowd Control Freeze Effect Core Skill

Ice Spear

Cooldown: 6s | Cost: 25 Energy

Launches ice spear to attack single enemy, dealing 220% attack power frost damage and applies slow effect, reducing movement speed by 50% for 4 seconds.

Single Target Slow Effect Common Skill

Blizzard

Cooldown: 45s | Cost: 80 Energy

Summons blizzard for 12 seconds, dealing 80% attack power frost damage per second to all enemies in area and continuously applying freeze and slow effects.

Ultimate Skill DoT Powerful Control

Shield Blessing

Cooldown: 20s | Cost: 50 Energy

Provides magic shield to all teammates, absorbing damage equal to 200% of Ice Mage's attack power for 8 seconds. Teammates gain 15% attack speed buff while shield exists.

Team Support Protection Buff Effect

Passive Skills

Frost Affinity

Ice attribute skill damage increased by 30%, freeze effect duration extended by 1 second.

Mana Regeneration

Recovers 2% max energy per second, 20% chance to not consume energy when casting skills.

Cold Domain

Enemies within 5 meters have 20% reduced movement speed, Ice Mage gains 15% damage reduction within this range.
