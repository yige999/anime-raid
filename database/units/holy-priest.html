Active Skills

Healing Light (Healing Light)

Cooldown: 5s | Cost: 25 Energy

Restore 250% of healing power to target ally. Additional 50% healing to targets below 50% HP.

Single Target Emergency Heal Core Skill

Holy Blessing (Holy Blessing)

Cooldown: 12s | Cost: 45 Energy

Apply holy blessing to all allies for 8 seconds, increasing healing received by 30% and providing damage immunity for 1 second.

Group Buff Immunity Team Support

Divine Shield (Divine Shield)

Cooldown: 20s | Cost: 60 Energy

Create divine shield on target ally that absorbs damage equal to 400% of healing power. Lasts 10 seconds or until destroyed.

Protection Damage Absorb Emergency Save

Holy Barrier (Holy Barrier)

Cooldown: 30s | Cost: 70 Energy

Create holy barrier for 12 seconds that reduces all incoming damage to allies inside by 40% and continuously heals them.

Ultimate Skill Area Protection Team Defense

Passive Skills

Light Affinity (Light Affinity)

Light element healing increased by 25%. Dark element damage taken reduced by 20%.

Energy Regeneration (Energy Regeneration)

Energy regeneration increased by 3% per second. Healing skills cost 15% less energy.

Holy Protection (Holy Protection)

All allies within 10m range receive 10% damage reduction and 5% healing boost.

Miracle Worker (Miracle Worker)

When an ally falls below 15% HP, automatically heal them for 200% of healing power (once per 60 seconds).
